My top 3 movies of all the time.

Lion King

Disney's The Lion King is about a young lion prince named Simba who, after losing his father in a staged accident which he feels he caused, must face his fear of responsibility and take his rightful place on the throne.

Forest Gump

Forrest Gump is a simple man with a low I.Q. but good intentions. He is running through childhood with his best and only friend Jenny.

Gone in 60 Seconds

A retired master car thief must come back to the industry and steal fifty cars with his crew in one night to save his brother's life.
